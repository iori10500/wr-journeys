Day by Day · 逐日行程
第1–2天 · 长沙
Days 1–2 · Changsha Arrival & Culture
D1
Day 1
新加坡 → 长沙 · 抵达 & 入住尼依格罗
Singapore → Changsha · Arrival at Niccolo
乘航班飞抵长沙黄花国际机场，专车接机前往梅溪湖畔的尼依格罗酒店（梅溪湖国际文化艺术中心旁）。下午在梅溪湖畔散步，傍晚前往屋顶酒吧欣赏金色夕阳与城市天际线。晚餐可选酒店米其林主厨料理。

Arrive Changsha Huanghua Airport, private transfer to Niccolo Changsha on Meixi Lake. Afternoon stroll along the lake promenade. Sundowner cocktails at the rooftop bar as the city lights up. Optional Michelin-level dinner in-house.
私家车接机 尼依格罗入住 梅溪湖 屋顶日落
D2
Day 2
长沙文化半日 · 驱车前往张家界
Changsha Culture Morning → Drive to Zhangjiajie
上午漫步橘子洲头（毛泽东青年雕像，湘江对望），或探访超级文和友市集体验本地街食文化。午后驱车约4小时前往武陵源，沿途欣赏湘西丘陵地貌。傍晚抵达悦榕庄张家界，晚餐于无边际泳池畔就餐，首次感受砂岩峰林的轮廓线。

Morning at Orange Isle (Young Mao sculpture, panoramic Xiangjiang views) or local culture at Chaoyang cultural market. ~4-hour private drive through the Xiangxi hills to Wulingyuan. Check in at Banyan Tree Zhangjiajie, dinner by the infinity pool as the pillar mountains fade into silhouette.

D3
Day 3
武陵源核心区 · 阿凡达山峰
Wulingyuan Core Area · Avatar Mountains
清晨搭乘百龙天梯（世界最高户外电梯，326米）登顶观景台，感受云海中砂岩柱林的震撼全景。上午步行十里画廊（电动观光车穿越峰林长廊）。下午乘坐缆车游览天子山，俯瞰"神堂湾"地貌。傍晚返回悦榕庄，SPA放松。

Take the Bailong Elevator (world's tallest outdoor lift, 326m) to sunrise at the summit. Walk the Ten-Mile Gallery by electric tram through pillar-lined corridors. Afternoon cable car to Tianzi Mountain and the Shentang Valley panorama. Evening Banyan Tree Spa.
百龙天梯 十里画廊 天子山 悦榕庄SPA
D4
Day 4
黄龙洞 · 金鞭溪徒步
Yellow Dragon Cave · Golden Whip Stream Hike
上午探秘黄龙洞，国家4A级溶洞群，地下宫殿般的石笋石柱（全程约1.5小时）。下午享受最轻松惬意的金鞭溪步道（7.5公里，溪边漫步穿越峰林，偶遇野生猕猴）。返回酒店享用悦榕庄星级晚餐。

Morning: Yellow Dragon Cave — vast underground halls of stalactites and stalagmites (1.5h tour). Afternoon: the gentle Golden Whip Stream trail (7.5km, flat, creek-side walk through the pillars, wild macaques likely spotted). Banyan Tree gourmet dinner.
黄龙洞 金鞭溪 野生猕猴 星级晚餐
Day by Day · 逐日行程
第5–6天 · 天门山 & 凤凰
Days 5–6 · Tianmen Mountain & Fenghuang Ancient Town
D5
Day 5
天门山玻璃栈道 → 下午出发凤凰
Tianmen Mountain Glass Skywalk → Drive to Fenghuang
上午前往张家界市区，乘坐世界最长索道（7455米）上天门山。挑战玻璃栈道（1600米悬挂式玻璃步道，深渊俯瞰）；漫步云梯（1000余台阶穿云而上），感受天门洞的壮阔。午后驱车约2.5小时抵达凤凰古城，办理入住精品沱江民宿，黄昏沿虹桥漫步。

Take the world's longest cable car (7,455m) up Tianmen Mountain. Walk the Glass Skywalk (1,600m glass-floored cliff walk) and ascend the Cloud Ladder through mist. Drive ~2.5h to Fenghuang Ancient Town. Check in at boutique Tuojiang riverside inn. Twilight stroll across Rainbow Bridge.
天门山索道 玻璃栈道 专车凤凰 沱江民宿入住
D6
Day 6
凤凰古城深度游 · 沱江夜泛
Fenghuang In Depth · Evening Boat on Tuojiang
上午8点前在朱砂古镇晨光中感受老街空巷（避开旅游人潮的最佳时段）。参观沈从文故居，感受《边城》文字背后的湘西气息。下午在苗族银匠铺体验手工银饰定制，可在苗族刺绣铺选购纹样。傍晚登上城楼观赏落霞，夜乘乌篷船游览华灯初上的沱江，灯火倒影令人难忘。

Early morning in Zhusha Ancient Town before crowds arrive. Visit Shen Congwen's Former Residence (China's greatest Hunan author). Afternoon: Miao silverwork workshop — custom pendant or bracelet made while you watch. Sunset from the city walls, then an evening punt along the lantern-lit Tuojiang.
朱砂古镇 沈从文故居 苗银定制 沱江夜泛
D7
Day 7
凤凰 → 长沙 · 文化尾声
Fenghuang → Changsha · Cultural Finale
上午在古城闲逛，早市采购凤凰姜糖、苗族腊肉等特产。午后专车约3.5小时返回长沙，抵达后前往湖南省博物馆（辛追娭毑汉代文物，马王堆出土丝绸，高度推荐）或在太平街拍照留念。晚餐必选长沙臭豆腐 + 辣椒炒肉，感受湘菜真正的辣度。

Morning browsing the old market (ginger candy, Miao cured meats). Drive ~3.5h to Changsha. Visit the Hunan Provincial Museum (Lady Dai's 2,000-year-old intact mummy and Han-dynasty silk burial garments — unmissable). Evening: street food finale at Taiping Street — stinky tofu and pork with chilies.
古城早市 专车长沙 湖南省博物馆 湘菜晚餐
D8
Day 8
长沙 → 新加坡 · 返程
Changsha → Singapore · Departure
根据航班时间，上午可在梅溪湖附近悠然享用早餐，或前往步步高广场购物。专车送往长沙黄花国际机场，乘航班返回新加坡。此刻行囊里已装满了悬浮山峰的照片、苗族银饰、以及凤凰古城傍晚的记忆。

Morning at leisure near Meixi Lake — final breakfast, last-minute souvenirs. Private transfer to Changsha Huanghua Airport. Fly home to Singapore, bags full of photos of floating mountains, Miao silver, and the memory of lantern light on the Tuojiang River.

Landscapes · 自然奇观
湖南的山河秘境
Hunan's Hidden Natural Wonders
武陵源砂岩峰林
Wulingyuan Sandstone Pillars
超过3,000根砂岩柱群，原型启发电影《阿凡达》。被誉为"地球上最像外星的景观"。Inspiration for Avatar's Hallelujah Mountains — 3,000+ sandstone columns rising from subtropical forest.
天门山玻璃栈道
Tianmen Glass Skywalk
悬挂于海拔1400米峭壁上的玻璃步道，总长1600米。脚下是千米深渊。1,600m of glass-floored walkway at 1,400m altitude — one of the world's most vertiginous walks.
凤凰古城
Fenghuang Ancient Town
中国保存最完好的苗族聚居古城，以沱江两岸的吊脚楼闻名。作家沈从文的故乡。China's finest preserved Miao-minority ancient town, immortalised by novelist Shen Congwen.
沱江夜色
The Tuojiang River at Night
华灯初上，红灯笼与吊脚楼倒映沱江，乘乌篷船穿越灯火，是凤凰最动人的时刻。Lanterns and stilt houses reflected on the Tuojiang — best experienced from a slow wooden punt at dusk.
